About Us

The King's Barber
Experience

Located in the heart of Wellingborough on Market Street, King's Barber brings the finest tradition of Turkish barbering combined with modern techniques and styling.

Our skilled barbers specialise in hot towel shaves, precision fades, and expert beard grooming. Every visit is an experience - from the moment you sit in our chair to the final brush-off.

We pride ourselves on delivering exceptional cuts in a welcoming, relaxed atmosphere. Walk-ins always welcome.

Whether you're after a classic gentleman's cut or the latest skin fade, our team has the expertise to deliver exactly what you're looking for.
